V10 Engine CAD Design

SolidWorks Design Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on the design and packaging of a naturally aspirated V10 engine using SolidWorks. Inspired by high-performance motorsport engines, the work emphasised realistic component layout, structural mounting considerations, and integration within a vehicle engine bay.

Iterative Design

Design Process

The project began with research into existing V10 engine architectures to establish realistic proportions and component relationships. Individual components were modelled parametrically before being assembled into a full engine layout. Iterative design changes were made to resolve packaging constraints, mounting interfaces, and assembly feasibility. Particular attention was given to alignment, tolerancing assumptions, and the interaction between rotating and stationary components.
