Carbon Fiber Skinning

Overview

This project involved applying carbon fiber skinning to Porsche GT3 RS components, including aerofoil mounts, ventilation pieces, and an engine cover. The aim was both aesthetic enhancement and improved durability.

Process Summary

  • Surface preparation and sanding
  • Carbon fabric layout and trimming
  • Epoxy application and curing
  • Final sanding and high-gloss finishing
