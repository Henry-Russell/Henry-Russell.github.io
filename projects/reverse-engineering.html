Reverse Engineering

Overview

This project involved the reverse engineering of automotive suspension and engine mounting components using 3D scanning and SolidWorks modelling. The goal was to create accurate CAD references for future integration of a V10 engine into a hypercar chassis.

Techniques Used

  • 3D optical scanning
  • Mesh cleanup and conversion to solid geometry
  • Dimensional verification and tolerance analysis
  • Creation of parametric CAD models
