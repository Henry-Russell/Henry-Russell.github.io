V10 Engine CAD Design Project

Overview

Full engine and engine bay CAD design inspired by high-performance automotive applications. Focused on packaging constraints, mechanical layout, and realistic component integration using SolidWorks.

Key Skills

SolidWorks CAD
Complex Assemblies
Design for Packaging
Mechanical Layout
Engineering Documentation

Process

Started with component sketches and exploded diagrams. Progressed to 3D assembly modelling, integrating constraints and motion simulations. Iterative testing ensured optimal fit and manufacturability.

Carbon Fiber Skinning Project

Overview

Composite manufacturing project involving wet layup carbon fiber skinning of performance vehicle components. Emphasis on surface preparation, fibre alignment, and quality finishing.

Key Skills

Composite Manufacturing
Wet Layup
Carbon Fibre Materials
Surface Preparation
Quality Control

Process

Prepared molds and layered carbon fiber sheets using wet layup techniques. Cured composites under controlled conditions, then finished surfaces with sanding and resin sealing for durability and aesthetics.

Systems Dynamics & Control Modelling

Overview

Analytical and simulation-based project focused on modelling dynamic systems using transfer functions, Laplace transforms, and time/frequency response analysis.

Key Skills

System Modelling
Laplace Transforms
MATLAB
Control Theory
Dynamic Analysis

Process

Developed mathematical models using Laplace transforms and transfer functions. Simulated responses in MATLAB, validated models, and iteratively refined system parameters for accurate dynamic prediction.
